Palakkad

Palakkad is one of the fourteen districts of Kerala and has no coastal line. The district opens the state to the rest of the country through the Palakkad Gap with a width of 32 to 40 Kms. The district is one of the main granaries of Kerala and its economy is primarily agricultural. Silent valley is a national park which is famous for its rare flaura and fauna. Tipu sultans fort situated right in the center of palakkad town is also another tourist attraction. The kalpathy rath yathra is another famous celebration that happens in the month of november. Nemmara Vellangi temple festivals are famous for its ear deafening fireworks. The peculiar malayalam accent used in palakkad is also famous.

Important places to visit

Parambikulam Wildlife Sanctuary
Malampuzha Dam
Silent Valley National Park
Palakkad Fort
